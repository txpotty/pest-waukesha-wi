Presence of Mosquito Larvae in Standing Water

Mosquitoes lay their eggs in stagnant water. If you notice small, wriggling creatures in bird baths, puddles, or even clogged gutters, these are likely mosquito larvae. This indicates active breeding on your property. Eliminating these breeding sites is a crucial step in breaking the mosquito life cycle and preventing future infestations. Our technicians are experts at identifying and treating these areas.

Buzzing Sounds Around Your Home

The high-pitched whine of a mosquito is a familiar and unwelcome sound. If you hear a constant buzzing around your windows, doors, or outdoor living spaces, it signals a heavy mosquito presence. This is not only annoying but also increases your risk of being bitten. Professional mosquito control targets these adult mosquitoes where they rest, significantly reducing their numbers.

Increased Mosquito Activity During Dawn and Dusk

Mosquitoes are most active during the cooler hours of dawn and dusk. If you notice a surge in mosquito activity during these times, it indicates a thriving mosquito population on your property.
